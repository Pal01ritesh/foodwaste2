Say No to food wastage!

In an eye-opening revelation, Indian households waste 50 kilograms of food per person per year or 68,760,163 tonnes a year., according to the United Nations Environment Programme's Food Waste Index Report 2022.In India, 40% of the food is wasted which is equivalent to Rs 92,000 crore a year.

Want to know more about us?

Our organisation is a Not-For-Profit surplus food management organisation connecting untouched edible surplus food with needy people to overcome the World Hunger

Here in our organisation we focus on managing food in such a way so that its wasteage can be reduced and the one's who truly needs it get the benefit of the same.
